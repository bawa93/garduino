Unique Product
Flora.
FLORA is a plant monitoring sytem. It analysis your plant and make sure every thing is suitable for complete growth of your plant.

Key Features

Key Features
Within FLORA we try to make everything easy to understand. We follow CAT theory, which is Care for your plant, Analyse your plant and keep Track of it. All the information is produced in an legible format so, you can bond with your plant in a well manner.
  1. Analyse the growth of plant with different sensors.
  2. Use of Arduino sensors for an accurate results.
  3. Consist of sensors for Humidity, Soil Fertility, Sunlight.
  4. Easy to use - with just plug and run support.
  5. Gives data of your plant in multiple graph formats.

Graphs

Graphs and You
We use the technology which uses modernise ways to show data and keep track of it. In FLORA we use graphical representations to show accurate plant data, so, you do not have to get paranoid about numbers and can easily understand what is going on with your plant. The graphs will show you live data and keep record of all the data from the first time your plant gets connected with flora. Make FLORA another root for your plant. The device will get connected with your plant by direct plugging it and it will start collecting data. You can check all the data by logging in your account on our website https://www.flora.ca
Humidity

Sunlight

Fertility
